About My Beauty Products

Welcome to My Beauty Products — a place built by beauty lovers, for beauty lovers. We started this site because we know how overwhelming it can be to find products that actually work without breaking the bank. There are thousands of serums, moisturizers, palettes, and tools out there, and honestly, most of them aren't worth your money. We're here to help you skip the noise.

What We Do

Our small editorial team spends hours every week researching, testing, and comparing beauty products across every category — skincare, makeup, haircare, and everything in between. We dig into ingredients, read real user reviews, and track pricing so you don't have to. When we recommend something, it's because we genuinely believe it delivers results at a fair price.

  • Honest, research-backed product recommendations
  • Focus on quality and value — not hype
  • Regularly updated picks to reflect new launches and price changes
  • Content written by people who actually use these products daily

Our Team

We're a tight-knit group of beauty enthusiasts with backgrounds in skincare science, cosmetics retail, and editorial writing. Between us, we've spent over a decade helping people find the right products for their skin, hair, and budget. This isn't a side project — it's something we care deeply about.

Transparency Matters

We earn a small commission through affiliate links, which means when you purchase a product through our site, we may receive a small percentage at no extra cost to you. This is what keeps My Beauty Products running and allows us to continue doing what we love. Our recommendations are never influenced by commissions — if a product isn't great, we won't feature it. Simple as that.

Thanks for trusting us with your beauty routine. We're glad you're here.
