How to Build Your Perfect Routine

Expert tips to help you choose the right products for your skin and hair

1

Know Your Skin Type First

Before investing in any skincare, figure out whether your skin is oily, dry, combination, or sensitive. A product that's holy grail for one person can be a disaster for another. When in doubt, CeraVe and The Ordinary are safe starting points for most skin types.

2

Invest Where It Counts

Splurge on items that stay on your skin (serums, moisturizers, sunscreen) and save on things you rinse off (cleansers, shampoos). A great SPF like Supergoop! Unseen Sunscreen is always worth the price.

3

Introduce Products One at a Time

Add one new product every two weeks so you can spot what's working and what's causing irritation. This is especially important with actives like BHA exfoliants or retinoids.

4

Don't Sleep on Hair Care

Your hair routine matters just as much as skincare. If you heat-style regularly, a bond-building treatment like Olaplex No. 3 can prevent long-term damage and keep hair looking salon-fresh.
